Welcome to ClaimSense AI Dashboard

This platform executes explainable machine learning models to detect fraud, inflated billing, and tariff upcoding for Indian health insurance claims. By converting SHAP (SHapley Additive exPlanations) values into conversational natural language reasons, we help claim review teams make faster, defensible decisions.

Core Features Checklist

  • Explainable Risk Scoring: Dynamic scoring based on procedure, hospital history, and room rent caps.
  • SHAP Attribution Visualization: Visualizes factors pushing the score up or down.
  • Interactive Review Actions: Approve, reject, or auto-adjust billing leakage directly from the details panel.
  • B2B API Simulator: Live-simulate claim queues to evaluate system scale and dashboard speed.
